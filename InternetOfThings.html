Internet of Things

What is the internet of things (IOT)?

The internet of things refers to a network of objects that are able to communicate with each other by passing data over the internet.

Examples of IOT:

  • A thermostat and radiator could communicate with each other such that if the room temperature drops below a threshold then the radiator will turn on
  • A clock and light could communicate with each other such that if the time goes past a certain hour in the night then the lights turn off
  • A door and burglar alarm could communicate with each other such that if the door is locked then the alarm is activated
